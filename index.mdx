## What is Lichess?

Lichess (li[chess in sca]la) is a free online chess game server focused on realtime gameplay and ease of use. With over 4.7 billion games in its database, Lichess serves millions of chess players worldwide completely free of charge, with no ads.

## Key Features

- **4.7+ billion games** stored in MongoDB and indexed by Elasticsearch
- **140+ languages** supported through community translations
- **Fully asynchronous** using Scala Futures and Akka streams
- **Distributed analysis** with Stockfish via the Fishnet cluster
- **Free PGN database** with all rated games published openly
- **Mobile apps** for iOS and Android
- **Zero ads, forever free** supported by donations
